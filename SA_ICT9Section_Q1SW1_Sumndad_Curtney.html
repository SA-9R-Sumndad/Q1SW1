My Manga/Manhwa/Manhua Collection

About last year, I started to collect mangas/manhwas. Now you might be wondering "What is the difference of mangas, manhwas, and manhuas?". Well, let me inform you. Mangas are the root of anime. It is read from right-to-left and top-to-bottom, is black and white, and is Japanese. Manhwa, on the other hand, is the root of some kdramas and hanguk aeni or korean animation. It is read from left-to-right and top-to-bottom, is colored, and is Korean. And lastly, Manhua is the root of some cdramas and donghua. It is read from right-to-left and top-to-bottom, is colored, and is Chinese. I don't really have any manhuas but I do have mangas and manhwas. They are mostly accessable online. Now that you know the differences of each type, let me tell you why I love collecting them and reccomend some of my favorites.

Why I Love Collecting and Reading Mangas/Manhwas/Manhuas

  • Collecting makes me happy, especially if it is completed.
  • Books are not really interesting to me, since I can't imagine it properly. Having those panels help me understand and get invested in the story more.
  • I read them when I have nothing to do in school or waiting for my fetcher.
  • It does not hurt my eyes, like when I read online or watch the adaptations on my phone.
  • In the mangas/manhwas/manhuas, it is usually the real and original story. In the adaptations, they cut out some interesting scenes making the story incomplete.
  • It satisfies my curiousity, since many adaptations are not yet finished. Reading it makes me know what's next.
  • It looks very aesthetic on my bookselves.

My Favorite Mangas

  1. Blue Lock - a soccer/shounen manga that focuses on the player's ego
  2. Toilet-Bound Hanako-kun - a horror manga where a girl befriends a ghost and goes on many adventures
  3. Oshi No Ko - a isekai manga where a doctor gets reincarnated as the child of an idol and plans to get revenge on whoever killed his mother
  4. A Sign of Affection - a shoujo manga where a deaf girl falls in love with a boy who loves to travel around the world
  5. Sasaki and Miyano - a BL manga where the main character loves to read BL manga but it never crossed his mind to question his sexuality until he met his senior

My Favorite Manhwas/Manhuas

  1. Who Made Me A Princess - a historical manhwa where a girl gets reincarnated into a novel that she had read in her past life where her character gets killed by her father
  2. Tower of God - an action manhwa that focuses on a boy who went on a journey to climb a tower full of other competetors in order to search for his friend
  3. Hidden Love : Can't Be Concealed - a romance manhwa where a girl fell in love with her brother's friend but plans to hide her feelings for him

The past year of collecting mangas/manhwas/manhuas was very exciting and entertaining. Everytime time I go to a mall, I always try to find a bookstore like National Book Store and Fully Booked. It makes me proud that I can save up and get the books that I want. I will continue to buy more books and grow my collection.
